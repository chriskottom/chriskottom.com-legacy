If you’re already serious about testing or feel it’s time to get serious, then this is the book for you. In it, you’ll learn:

  • How to structure your tests and specs effectively
  • How to use mocks and stubs (and when to avoid them)
  • Ways to clean out cruft and configure your tests for maximum performance
  • Which supporting gems and plugins are available for building your ultimate test stack
  • How to make the switch from other frameworks to Minitest
  • How to extend the framework and bend it to your will!
