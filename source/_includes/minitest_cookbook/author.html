About the author

Hi, I'm Chris Kottom, and I've been building web applications since the mid-90s. When I first discovered Rails in 2006, I'd been a Java developer for over ten years and was pretty burned out by the experience. Discovering Ruby reminded me how much I loved coding and brought me back to it.

I came to Ruby with almost no previous experience writing automated tests, but I was immediately able to see the value in terms of software quality and programmer productivity. Like a lot of longtime Rubyists, I've used plenty of different testing frameworks over the years - Test::Unit, Shoulda, RSpec, and now finally arriving at Minitest. I'm writing this book because I think I've finally found a way of testing that's reliable and that I can live with long-term. I hope I can share that with other developers and help them do the same.
